Blackjack

Blackjack, also known as 21, is a classic card game that has stood the test of time. It's a game of strategy and skill where your goal is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. The rules are simple: you're dealt two cards, and you can choose to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep your current hand). The excitement builds as you aim for that magical 21 or outsmart the dealer. With multiple variations and betting options, Blackjack offers endless possibilities for both beginners and experts.
